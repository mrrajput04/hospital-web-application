about us

we take care of your healthy life

By the Vision of Vivek Group(Est.2002) of serving the society as their family and to facilitate them with each and every Medical services in the region of the District,Under the Guidance of Mr.Amit Goel (Chairman) Vivek Healthcare and Wellness centre is a Super Speciality Tertiary Care Hospital with all advance and state of art Equipments to overcome the burden on healthcare with the Excellent Clinical and Critical care management in the reign with the motive of Bringing perfection in healing, Vivek Hospital foundation was laid with the Mission to Provide affordable and Quality healthcare services to the people of Bijnor and nearby Places with the best possible efforts to serve the society in any manner of Modern Sciences and wellness approach, and Holding a Vision of Developing this institute as an Advance Medical College in the Region.
