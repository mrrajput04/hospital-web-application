Without a clearly defined Mission and Vision the Plans can go in any direction and the management does not have a clear road ahead of them. Mission and Vision are there to define the strategic direction of the organization. Mission statement should answer the question that Why do we exist? Ultimately every organization is created by the owners to create wealth for them but this cannot be the organization goal. How this wealth would be created would be the mission statement. The mission statement should be the guiding principle for every employee of the organization. The mission statement answers the basic questions of why your company exists and describes the needs your company was created to fulfill.
